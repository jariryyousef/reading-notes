# What Google Learned From Its Quest to Build the Perfect Team

- What Google learned about building the perfect team?


Psychological safety: Everyone feels safe in taking risks around their team members, and that they won't be embarrassed or punished for doing so. Dependability: Everyone completes quality work on time. Structure and clarity: Everyone knows what their specific expectations are.   

- How do you build a successful team?

1. Set SMART goals.
2. Perform well-defined roles.
3. Experiment regularly.
4. Embrace diversity.
5. Share a common culture.
6. Be accountable to the team.
7. Communicate effectively.
8. Welcome strong leadership.

- What is team building Google?


Google recognized the many benefits of high-performance teamwork in a software engineering environment and committed to building the perfect team. In 2012 they launched Project Aristotle, an initiative to analyze teams and identify which factors contributed to success (and which ones did not) 

- What makes a high-performing team Google? 
Google conducted a two-year study and concluded that high-performing teams have five specific traits: Psychological safety: The willingness to take risks without feeling insecure or embarrassed. Dependability: Delivering high-quality work on time. Structure & clarity: Clarity of goals, roles, and execution plans.
